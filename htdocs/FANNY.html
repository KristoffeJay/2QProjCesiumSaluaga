Hero Feature

An agile assassin that can connect her cables to walls that allows her to dive into the enemy formation swiftly and lethally. [Hero Entrance: Sir, what's your command?]

Role Assassin
Specialty Chase/Reap
Laning Recommendation Jungle

Passive: Air Superiority (Buff)

According to her flying speed, Fanny's damage while flying is increased by 15% to 30% and leaves a Prey Mark on the target (Stack up to 2 times). For Each stack of Prey Mark the target has, Fanny regens 10 energy when she deals damage to it. (Energy regen effect decays if she deals damage to multiple enemy heroes in a short time.)

First Skill: Tornado Strike (AoE)(Physical Skill)

Fanny whirls her blade, dealing (depends on level) 320-520 (+80% Total Physical Attack) points of Physical Damage to nearby enemies. The skill has a 15 energy cost and a cooldown that scales per level; Cooldown (seconds): 3.5 / 3.3 / 3.1 / 2.9 / 2.7 / 2.5.

Second Skill: Steel Cable (Blink)(Charge Skill)

Fanny throws out a cable that makes her fly to the first obstacle it touches. Each successive use within 2 seconds decreases Energy Cost by 1 and changes her flying direction. If energy is sufficient and she hits an enemy while flying, Tornado Strike (1st skill) will be triggered. This skill has 0 sec cooldown. Its energy cost per level is: 17 / 16 / 15 / 14 / 13 / 12

Ultimate Skill: Cut Throat (Burst/Blink)(Physical Skill)

Fanny intiates a quick attack on an enemy, dealing 500-860 (+240% Extra Physical Attack)Physical Damage. If she hits an enemy with a Prey Mark, the damage will be increased by 20% per stack. Its cooldown reduces per level (seconds): 35 / 30 / 25, and has an energy cost of 30.

Hero Advantages and Disadvantages

Fanny is one of the most mobile and fast heroes in the game. Because of her 2nd skill, she can be too slippery and can also deal high damage in early and midgame because of her speed in farming and in killing heroes.

However, she has disadvantages, especially in late game. Just like Ling, she’s dependent on the blue buff (an advantageous buff that can be acquired by killing a certain jungle creep) because of how high her energy usage is. She’s reliant on the walls to use her cables (2nd skill). Fanny is one of the most difficult heroes to use in the game and requires high skills, good control, and fast hands to use effectively. She also has a lot of counter heroes that could stop her by using their CC/Displacement/Interrupt skills such as stuns and knockbacks.

Reasons in choosing this Hero

This hero is one of my favorites as she helped me climb up the ranks during her peak form when she was one of the meta heroes. She could easily kill squishy heroes and is super fun to use because of her speed and agility. She could go in and out of the turrets easily and is very good when diving and ganking. Even though she went through a lot of nerfs and it’s getting super hard to use her these days, she’s still one of my top picks.
